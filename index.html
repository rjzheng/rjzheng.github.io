Welcome!

Welcome to my site everyone. My name is Richard Zheng, and I am a Sophomore at UCSD studying Computer Engineering. Please feel free to learn more about me by clicking about, or browse through my projects.

A little about myself...

I am an American born Chinese, and spent half of my life in China as a child. Growing up in Shanghai, I learned to speak, read and type Mandarin and Shanghainese fluently. I came to America as a 4th grader. Although it was hard for me to adapt to a new living style and language, I am now completely fluent with English.

I have an older brother who is now a graduate student at Stanford, and received his undergraduate degree at MIT for electrical engineering. Under his influence, I gradually developed interest for Software development and coding in general. Hence, I am currently pursuing Computer Science/Engineering degree at University of California San Diego.

Aside from learning in school, I like to follow tutorials online and study from other programming books during my free time. I am constantly searching for ways to improve as a programmer.In fact, I am currently learning to make iOS apps with Xcode.

I hope to become successful in the field that I love, and I will devote all my effort to do so. I welcome any opportunities, so please contact me if you are ever interested to know more about me or work with me.

Clash of Clans Calculator

This is for all of the Clash of Clans fans out there!

Description:

     I am personally a big Clash of Clans fan, and I always want to make sure that I gain at least the same amount of elixer and dark elixer as what I spent to make troops. At the time, I couldn't find any Clash of Clan troops calculator available, so I made this app.

     This is a very simple Clash of Clan troops calculator which consists of all the available troops with different levels and the elixer and dark elixer costs. It also allows users to acquire the total elixer cost of each troop category, as well as the complete total by clicking the bottom right "calculate" button.

Language Used:

  • Python
  • Tkinter

    • Worry-Free Morning

    Nothing ever works better to start the day with!

    Description:

         This app is perfect for student or adults who have repetitive routines throughout the week. Are you tired of listening to the same alarm sounds built-in in iPhones? Are you annoyed by needing to check the weather, and traffic from home to work everyday? Do you want to see your daily schedule first thing in the morning? If you answered yes to any of the above questions, then this app would be perfect for you!!

         This app allows you to create different accounts for different schedules. If you have school tomorrow and work the day after, then you can simply log into different accounts without having to change your settings everytime. You also don't have to worry about other people having access to your information, because this app requires the users to sign in with their own passwords.

     

     

    Features:

  • Alarm
  • Weather
  • Maps/Directions (Google)
  • Calendar/To-do list (Google)
